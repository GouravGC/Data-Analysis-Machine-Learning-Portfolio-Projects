## Indian Startups Funding Analysis

📊 This project explores Top 15 Sectors by Total Funding , Total Startup Funding in India by Year , Top 15 Investors by Total Investment , Most Frequent Startup Names.

- 📁 Dataset: Startup Funding Dataset
- 🛠 Tools: Numpy, Pandas, Seaborn, Matplotlib, Plotly
- 📈 Plots: Top 15 Sectors by Total Funding , Total Startup Funding in India by Year , Top 15 Investors by Total Investment , Most Frequent Startup Names.
