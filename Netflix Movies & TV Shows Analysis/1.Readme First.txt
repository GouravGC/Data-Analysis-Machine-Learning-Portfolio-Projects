## Retail Sales Analysis

📊 This project explores Top Genres by Count (Interactive Bar Chart) , Movie vs TV Show Split by Year , Top Countries Producing Netflix Content , WordCloud (Plotly-style: Convert to Image) , Interactive Pie Chart of Content Types , Top 10 Directors on Netflix , Distribution of Movie Durations , Distribution of TV Show Seasons , Monthly Netflix Content Additions Over Time

- 📁 Dataset: Netflix titles public free dataset
- 🛠 Tools: Numpy , Pandas, Seaborn , Matplotlib , Plotly
- 📈 Plots: Top Genres by Count (Interactive Bar Chart) , Movie vs TV Show Split by Year , Top Countries Producing Netflix Content , WordCloud (Plotly-style: Convert to Image) , Interactive Pie Chart of Content Types , Top 10 Directors on Netflix , Distribution of Movie Durations , Distribution of TV Show Seasons , Monthly Netflix Content Additions Over Time
