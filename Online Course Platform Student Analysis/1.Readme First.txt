## Online Course Platform Student Analysis

📊 This project explores Completion Rate by Course , Average Quiz Score per Course , Quiz Score vs Time Spent on Video , Average Engagement (Drop-off Point) by Course , Drop-off Timestamp Heatmap by Course using Simulate data.

- 📁 Dataset: Simulate dataset
- 🛠 Tools: Numpy , Pandas , Seaborn , Matplotlib , Plotly
- 📈 Plots: Completion Rate by Course , Average Quiz Score per Course , Quiz Score vs Time Spent on Video , Average Engagement (Drop-off Point) by Course , Drop-off Timestamp Heatmap by Course
