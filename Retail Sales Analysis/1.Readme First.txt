## Retail Sales Analysis

📊 This project explores Monthly Sales Trend , Sales By Region , Profit By Category , Sales By Product , Sum Of Sales By Year , Sum Of Profit By Category , Sum Of Quantity By Category

- 📁 Dataset: Kaggle Bangalore , Pune , Delhi dataset
- 🛠 Tools: Numpy , Pandas, Seaborn , Matplotlib , Plotly
- 📈 Plots: Made Through Tableu/Power Bi - Monthly Sales Trend , Sales By Region , Profit By Category , Sales By Product , Sum Of Sales By Year , Sum Of Profit By Category , Sum Of Quantity By Category
